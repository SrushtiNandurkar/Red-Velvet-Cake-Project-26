# ingredients
Ingredients for Making a Red Velvet cake are as follows:
1) Cake
2) Red food colouring
3) Cream cheese
4) Icing
5) Buttermilk
6) Butter
7) Flour
8) Sugar
9) Egg
10) Vinegar
11) Cocoa Solids
12) Powdered Sugar
13) Baking Powder
14) Vanilla Extract
15) All Purpose Flour
16) Unsalted Butter
